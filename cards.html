Squid Game Challenges

This is Minecraft Squid Game! You may bring you friends (max of 16) or host your public server with this map that includes credits and asking walkndounuts for permission on the Discord server. All 6 games are from both season 1 and 2. These games are fully functional with command blocks. Though some of them will need a bit of fixing so soon I will update some of them. The six games include:

Red Light Green Light

Navigate the deadly field by stopping on red and running on green. Classic and dangerous.

Dalgona Cookies

Form teams and out-pull your opponents over the pit. Strategy and strength decide the winner.

Glass Bridge

Choose the right panel or fall to your doom. Pure luck or memorize the path!

Mingle

Choose the right panel or fall to your doom. Pure luck or memorize the path!

Six-Legged Pentathlon

Choose the right panel or fall to your doom. Pure luck or memorize the path!

Squid Game

Choose the right panel or fall to your doom. Pure luck or memorize the path!

(random bonus) Blood Night

Choose the right panel or fall to your doom. Pure luck or memorize the path!
